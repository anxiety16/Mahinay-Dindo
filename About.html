Welcome to Coffee Break cofe


Welcome to Coffee Break Café, where every visit is a moment to pause, relax, and recharge. Founded by friends who share a love for coffee and community, we’ve created a cozy, rustic space that feels like home. With warm lighting, handcrafted decor, and the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee, our café is a haven from the everyday rush.


At Coffee Break Café, we’re committed to sustainability and local partnerships, ensuring that each cup supports both the environment and our community. Beyond just coffee, we host events that celebrate local talent, making our café a hub for creativity and connection.


Join us at Coffee Break Café, where every sip is a break well deserved.

Team

Jhonny Mahinay

With years of experience behind the counter, Jhonny is a master of the art of coffee. His journey began as a young barista, where he fell in love with the complexities of coffee brewing. Over the years, Jhonny has honed his craft, developing a keen understanding of how to balance flavors and aromas to create the perfect cup. His passion for coffee is matched only by his commitment to sharing that experience with others, making every cup he serves a reflection of his dedication to quality and excellence.

Dindo Agustin

Dindo culinary journey started in his grandmother’s kitchen, where he learned the basics of baking. Inspired by his family’s love for food, he pursued a career in the culinary arts, quickly discovering his talent for pastry creation. Dindo’s approach to baking is a blend of tradition and innovation, combining classic techniques with modern flavors. His creativity shines through in every pastry he crafts, from timeless favorites to new, exciting creations. At Coffee Break Café, Dindo’s creations are not just desserts; they’re edible works of art that delight the senses.

Café's Mission


Our mission at Coffee Break Café is to create a cozy and welcoming environment where coffee enthusiasts can gather, relax, and enjoy exceptional coffee. We are dedicated to crafting high-quality beverages and pastries while fostering a sense of community and connection. Every visit to our café is designed to be a warm, inviting experience that brings people together over a shared love of coffee.
